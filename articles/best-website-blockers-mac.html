Finding the right website blocker for Mac can significantly improve your productivity and help you maintain focus. We've tested and reviewed the top website blocking applications available for macOS in 2025.

1. Cold Turkey Blocker - Best Overall

Price: Free / Pro version $39

Cold Turkey Blocker stands out as the most comprehensive website blocking solution for Mac users. The free version offers robust blocking capabilities, while the Pro version adds advanced scheduling and tamper-proof features.

Key Features:

  • Blocks websites, applications, and even your entire computer
  • Scheduled blocking sessions with recurring options
  • Tamper-proof protection prevents easy bypassing
  • Detailed usage statistics and reporting
  • Password protection for settings

Pros:

  • Completely free basic version
  • Extremely difficult to bypass when properly configured
  • Works across all browsers and applications
  • Regular updates and active development

Cons:

  • Interface can be overwhelming for beginners
  • Some advanced features require Pro version

2. Freedom - Best for Cross-Platform Users

Price: $3-7/month depending on plan

Freedom excels at providing consistent blocking across all your devices. If you work on multiple platforms, Freedom's synchronization capabilities make it an excellent choice.

Key Features:

  • Syncs across Mac, iOS, Windows, and Android
  • Pre-made blocklists for common distractions
  • Locked mode prevents session modifications
  • Detailed productivity insights
  • Team and family plans available

Pros:

  • Seamless cross-device synchronization
  • User-friendly interface
  • Excellent customer support
  • Regular feature updates

Cons:

  • Subscription-based pricing
  • Limited customization compared to Cold Turkey

3. Focus - Best for Simplicity

Price: $19.99 one-time purchase

Focus offers a clean, minimalist approach to website blocking. It's perfect for users who want effective blocking without complex configuration options.

Key Features:

  • Simple, intuitive interface
  • Pomodoro timer integration
  • Whitelist and blacklist modes
  • Productivity tracking
  • Notification blocking

Pros:

  • One-time purchase, no subscription
  • Very easy to set up and use
  • Lightweight and fast
  • Great for Pomodoro technique users

Cons:

  • Limited advanced features
  • Mac-only (no cross-platform sync)

4. 1Blocker - Best Safari Extension

Price: Free / Premium $4.99/month

While primarily known as an ad blocker, 1Blocker offers excellent website blocking capabilities specifically for Safari users.

Key Features:

  • Native Safari integration
  • Custom blocking rules
  • Privacy protection
  • Regular filter updates
  • iOS synchronization

Pros:

  • Excellent Safari performance
  • Also blocks ads and trackers
  • Syncs with iOS version
  • Regular updates

Cons:

  • Safari-only (doesn't work with Chrome, Firefox)
  • Limited application blocking

5. SelfControl - Best Free Option

Price: Free (open source)

SelfControl is a simple, open-source website blocker that's completely free and surprisingly effective for basic blocking needs.

Key Features:

  • Completely free and open source
  • Simple blacklist/whitelist functionality
  • Timer-based blocking sessions
  • Cannot be easily bypassed once started
  • Lightweight and reliable

Pros:

  • Completely free
  • Open source and transparent
  • Very reliable
  • No account registration required

Cons:

  • Basic interface and limited features
  • No scheduling or advanced options
  • Development has slowed in recent years

Choosing the Right Website Blocker

For comprehensive blocking: Choose Cold Turkey Blocker

For cross-device sync: Choose Freedom

For simplicity: Choose Focus

For Safari users: Choose 1Blocker

For budget-conscious users: Choose SelfControl

Tips for Maximum Effectiveness

  • Combine methods: Use both application-level and DNS-level blocking
  • Set realistic goals: Start with shorter blocking periods
  • Use accountability: Have someone else set up the blocker
  • Regular review: Update your blocked sites list monthly
  • Plan alternatives: Have productive activities ready when you feel the urge to browse

The best website blocker is the one you'll actually use consistently. Consider your specific needs, budget, and technical comfort level when making your choice.
